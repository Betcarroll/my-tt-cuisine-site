Provision & Saltfish

This dish is traditional consisting of boiled ground provisions (such as dasheen and green fig) served with saltfish which is typically sauteed with tomatoes, onions and seasonings

Doubles

Doubles is a popular street food made with two small, flat fried bread (bara) filled with curried chickpeas (chana), topped with various chutneys and sauces.

Roti

A roti/dhalpuri is a type of flatbread stuffed with seasoned ground split peas rolled thinly and cooked flat. Commonly used to wrap curried fillings like goat, chicken, potato & channa/chickpea serve in a wrap.

Sewaiyan

Sewaiyan is a sweet vermicelli pudding that is popular in Muslim communities, in Trinidad and Tobago. It is often served during festivals and celebrations, such as Eid-ul-Fitr.Sewaiyan is made by cooking thin vermicelli noodles (also known as seviyan or semiya) in sweetened milk, along with dried fruits like raisins,

Provision & Stew Beef

Provision and Stew Beef: A hearty stew made with various ground provisions (such as yams, sweet potatoes, and dasheen) and chunks of beef, simmered in a flavorful broth with seasonings and herbs.

Coo Coo Calallo & Stew Fish

Coo Coo Callaloo and Stew Fish: Coo Coo is a polenta-like dish made with cornmeal and okra, often served with callaloo (a leafy green vegetable) and stewed chicken/fish, which is braised with tomatoes, onions, and seasonings.

Bake and shark

Bake and Shark: A popular street food consisting of a fried shark fillet (or other fish) sandwiched between two pieces of fried bread (bake), often served with various condiments and sauces.

Mango & Pineapple chow

Chow is a flavorful mixed dish made with a combination of locally available fruits, such as the inclusion of green/unripe fruits like papaya and mangoes,pineapples, is what gives Trinidadian Chow its unique flavor, combining sweet, sour, and savory notes.

SweetBread

Sweetbread is a delicious, moist, sweet loaf made with grated coconut, raisins, cherries and flavored with spices like cinnamon and nutmeg.It is a traditional holiday treat.
