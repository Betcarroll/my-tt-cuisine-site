About Us

A brief history of the Taste of Trinidad and Tobago Cuisine.

The Caribbean has a food culture and it provides a venue for students, food cognisors, professionals in the culinary arts, and food experts to learn about and sample the variety of Trinidad and Tobago Cuisine. Its main purpose and intention is to showcase Trinidad and Tobago rich culinary legacy. We are dedicated to promote the diverse culinary heritage of Trinidad and Tobago. Throughout the years, we have conducted workshops, culinary tours, and other events to inform foodies about the cultural value of Trinidad and Tobago. Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the unique flavours and traditions that have shaped T&T rich culinary landscape. We are instrumental in promoting the country as a premier culinary destination, attracting visitors from around the globe to explore the vibrant and favourful cuisines of the Island.
